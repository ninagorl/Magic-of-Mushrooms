The Magic of
Mushrooms

Several clinical trials focused on mental illnesses like depression,
anxiety, and addiction have shown that psilocybin appears to
boost patients moods, even weeks after taking the drug. For ex-
ample, psilocybin appears to increase the brain's neuroplasticity-
the ability for neural networks to shift and rewire. In research with
people, psilocybin appears to make it easier to break out of habits
and become more adaptive. "It increases the capacity of the brain
to change, and therefore for thinking and behavior to change."

Mental Disorders

A mental disorder is characterized by a clinically significant disturbance in
an individual's cognition, emotional regulation, or behaviour. It is usually associated with distress or impairment in important areas of functioning.
There are many different types of mental disorders. Mental disorders may
also be referred to as mental health conditions.
